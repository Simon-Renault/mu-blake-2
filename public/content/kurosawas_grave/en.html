Kurosawa's grave

Kamakura, Japan. In Search of the grave of the emperor of cinema — of a spiritual encounter that has been waiting for years — it's in the middle of a partially destroyed cemetery that the confluence quickly takes cinematographic paces. The spirits wake up and tell, from the vestiges of the place, how remains the myth of the virtuoso director, having escaped from a natural disaster. All the elements of the cemetery manifest themselves in turn, fueling the desire to make this movie scene of recollection a real film set, including the cinematterial keys of reading which are already present — pending, as left in evidence by the deformations of time and space.
